"20 Years of Trust"

VEEYES DENTAL HOSPITAL is India's largest state-of-the-art dental facility, dedicated to delivering exceptional care. With over 20 years of excellence, Veeyes Dental Hospital remains a trusted name in dentistry.

Guided by our motto, "Healthy Teeth, Healthy Lifestyle," we strive to help every patient achieve optimal oral health.

As one of the first standalone dental hospitals in India to achieve NABH accreditation, we uphold the highest standards of safety and quality in dental care.

Our Journey of Excellence

2005

In 2005, Dr. Ramesh Shanmugam founded Sanjeevi Dental Clinic in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu. This 600-square-foot clinic quickly became known for its expertise in dental and facial surgeries, adopting cutting-edge technologies.

2012

In 2012, the practice expanded into VEEYES DENTAL CARE, with Dr. Ranjana Ramesh joining the team. A new branch was established in Coimbatore, growing into a 2,500-square-foot facility with six operatory rooms. Patients from across Tamil Nadu, including Erode, Ooty, and Salem, sought care here.

Today, VEEYES DENTAL HOSPITAL serves patients from across the state and internationally, providing advanced treatments for facial pain, oral complications, and long-term dental health.
