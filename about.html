Our Journey of Excellence

2005

In 2005, Dr. Ramesh Shanmugam founded Sanjeevi Dental Clinic in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu. This 600-square-foot clinic quickly became known for its expertise in dental and facial surgeries, adopting cutting-edge technologies.

2012

In 2012, the practice expanded into VEEYES DENTAL CARE, with Dr. Ranjana Ramesh joining the team. A new branch was established in Coimbatore, growing into a 2,500-square-foot facility with six operatory rooms. Patients from across Tamil Nadu, including Erode, Ooty, and Salem, sought care here.

2025

In 2022, we expanded into a 35,000-square-foot, fully digitalized facility. Key features include:

  • 27 dental operatory rooms with digital x-rays
  • A surgical operation theatre with an ICU
  • 15 inpatient rooms for extended care
  • In-house CAD-CAM and blood investigation labs
  • Advanced learning center, pharmacy, and canteen services

Our advanced diagnostic tools, including Digital OPG, CBCT, and intraoral scanners, ensure precise diagnoses and effective treatments. The CSSD (Central Sterile Supply Department) department enforces strict sterilization protocols for unparalleled hygiene and safety.

Today, VEEYES DENTAL HOSPITAL serves patients from across the state and internationally, providing advanced treatments for facial pain, oral complications, and long-term dental health.

Founder

Ramesh Shanmugam, M.D

Dr. Ramesh, a distinguished dental surgeon, has made substantial contributions to the fields of Orthodontics and Oral Implantology. With a rich background encompassing twenty-two years of experience in clinical, academic, research, and administrative roles, he has played a pivotal role in advancing dental care.

Ranjana Ramesh, Ph.D.

With over two decades of experience, Dr. Ranjana is a seasoned cosmetic dentist, surgeon, and bio functional Prosthetic Specialist. Initially practicing in Chennai, she has now extended her expertise to Coimbatore and Tirupur. Dr. Ranjana co-directs two leading dental specialty hospitals in these cities, offering comprehensive dental care for patients of all age groups. Her reputation extends beyond borders, attracting patients from across the country and around the world.

Specializing in whole mouth rehabilitation, laser dentistry, implant dentistry, aesthetic dentistry, and BPS (Biofunctional Prosthetic System), Dr. Ranjana brings a diverse range of skills to her practice. Actively contributing to current dental research and future trends, she has presented award-winning papers at various national and international dental conferences.
